From Brooklyn Neighbors to Global Movement

Our Origin Story

Brooklyn, 2006

In 2006, Brooklyn environmentalists worried about the clustering of pollution spewing businesses and contaminated properties around Newtown Creek began working with local groups; developing digital tools that enabled everyday people to share how pollution impacted their lives and demand a healthier environment.

Since then, HabitatMap has helped communities across the globe fight environmental injustice by providing accessible tools and expertise.
