From Brooklyn Neighbors to Global Movement

What started as a small group of concerned Brooklyn residents has grown into a worldwide network of communities fighting for environmental justice through accessible technology and data.

Our Origin Story

Brooklyn, 2006

In 2006, Brooklyn neighbors worried about their families' health near the polluted Newtown Creek. They could see and smell contamination but lacked data to prove its impact, so they started HabitatMap.

Since then, the initiative has helped communities across the globe fight environmental injustice by providing accessible tools and expertise.
