Technology for Environmental Justice

HabitatMap develops accessible tools that put environmental monitoring power directly into the hands of communities fighting for clean air.

AirBeam: Portable Air Quality Monitor

Our flagship device makes air quality monitoring accessible and affordable. Real-time data collection empowers communities with the evidence they need.

Mobile App Integration

Connect your AirBeam to our mobile app for real-time monitoring, data visualization, and community sharing.

Data Visualization

Transform complex air quality data into clear, actionable insights that communities can use for advocacy.

Community Networks

Build networks of community monitors to create comprehensive air quality maps and strengthen collective advocacy.

Policy Advocacy

Use collected data as evidence for policy change, regulatory enforcement, and environmental justice campaigns.
